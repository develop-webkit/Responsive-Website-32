PropUp- the solution to landlords headaches

So much rental management is still done over the phone and on paper. PropUp brings owning a rental into the now with secure payments, safe documentation and job automation saving you time and money.

Our Story

Luke and Aaron saw hard working landlords having to Spend their evenings and weekends making calls and running errands to keep their rentals running well. And those landlords that couldn't afford to spend the hours it took to manage a rental well had to pay Someone a chunk of mooney to do it for them.

L & A understood that most of what needed doing in order to be a great landlord was able to be automated. And so, they imagined PropUp. PropUp would work in the background to monitor and tend to the logistics involed in owning a rental property-it'd file the paperwork, calculate the payments, Schedule the maintenance, etc and then call on the landlord if and uhen they were needed.

After a year and a half of long nights and early moringss PropUp was a reality.
